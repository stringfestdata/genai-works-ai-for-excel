# Read‑Aloud Scripts

A say‑it‑straight script for every slide, written to be **read aloud**. Short
lines, one breath each, plenty of space. If your mind goes blank, just read the
lines top to bottom and you'll sound natural and on‑message.

Keep this open on a second screen (or print it). It maps slide‑for‑slide to the
Figma deck. You don't have to read it word‑for‑word — it's a safety net.

> Tip: each blank line is a pause. Don't rush them.

---

### Slide 9 — The recorded steps

Here's the part that matters.

See this "Applied Steps" list on the right? That's your entire workflow, written down.

### Slide 10 — Connect, don't paste

If you remember one thing from today, make it this.

A connection can be refreshed. A paste cannot.
